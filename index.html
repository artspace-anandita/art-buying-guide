01. Three Ways to Own This Artwork

Detailed Process

Whether you are looking for an exact digital replica or a one-of-a-kind textured original, we have three ways to bring art into your home:

🖼️

Canvas Print

A Canvas Print is a high-quality digital reproduction of the original artwork.

  • Printed on premium textured canvas
  • Captures colors and details with precision
  • Ideal for modern homes and gifting
  • Clean, consistent finish

Best for:
Those who want an affordable yet premium-looking artwork.

🎨

Hand Painted

A Hand Painted artwork is a carefully recreated version of the original piece.

  • I will personally recreate the piece on canvas
  • While it follows the original design, every brushstroke is unique, giving the piece a physical texture and "soul" that only a handmade painting has.

Best for:
Art lovers who value originality and texture.

✨

Commission

Custom-made for you. If you love a style but want something personal, let’s create it together.

  • Fully customized artwork based on your idea
  • Choose size ranging from A4 to A1, theme, colors, and style
  • Collaborative process (discussion + approval)

Best for:
Those who want a deeply personal or custom-designed artwork.

03. Print vs Hand-Painted

Print vs Hand-Painted — what's the real difference?

Both options use the same composition. Here's how they differ in feel, texture and ownership.

🖼️ Canvas Print (Precision & Consistency)

A high-fidelity digital reproduction of the original artwork, printed on premium archival-grade textured canvas. Every gradient, every brush mark, every detail is faithfully reproduced.

  • Printed on fine art canvas (Hahnemühle Daguerre or Felix Schoeller)
  • Colours are vibrant and UV-resistant for long-lasting display
  • Ideal if you want a perfect replica of the exact artwork shown
  • More accessible price point than a hand-painted piece
  • Suitable for gifting — arrives beautifully packaged

🎨 Hand Painted (Texture & Authenticity)

A true original — each piece is recreated entirely by hand on artist-grade canvas. No two pieces are exactly alike; you're receiving a one-of-a-kind artwork with real paint and real character.

  • Painted on Camel Artist Canvas Pad — artist-grade texture
  • Subtle variations in brushwork make it uniquely yours
  • Raised paint texture catches light beautifully on a wall
  • You're owning a piece of original creative craft
  • Higher value and a cherished heirloom for years to come

08. Everything you need to know

Frequently Asked Questions

Ordering & Process

How do I place an order — is there a checkout?
There's no automated checkout on this site. All orders are placed personally via WhatsApp or email (see footer below). This allows us to confirm details, discuss preferences, and ensure you're happy before work begins.
Can I cancel an order after placing it?
For Canvas Prints and Hand Painted pieces, cancellations can be accepted before work has begun. Once production starts, cancellations are not possible as materials and time have already been committed. For Commissions, cancellations before the sketch stage will receive a refund of the deposit. After the sketch is approved and painting has begun, the deposit is non-refundable.
Can I see the artwork before it ships?
Yes. For commissions, I share progress photos at the sketch and near-completion stages for your review. Final dispatch happens only after you're satisfied. For Canvas Prints and Hand Painted pieces, a photo of the finished work can be shared before packing if requested.

Product Information

Is the Canvas Print hand-painted?
No, these are digitally printed reproductions of original hand-painted artworks.
Will the Canvas Print look exactly like the preview?
There may be minor variations in color or detail due to differences between screens and printed output.
What does "Rolled Canvas with white border" mean?
Your artwork is printed or painted with a small white margin around the image — similar to a fine art print. This border is intended for framing or stretching, allowing the framer to mount the piece without cutting into the artwork itself.

Customization & Commissions

Can I customize the size?
For Canvas Prints and Hand Painted pieces, A3 is currently the standard size. For Commissions, sizes from A4 to A1 are available — just reach out and we'll discuss what works best for your space and budget.
For a commission — how many revisions do I get?
Each commission includes two rounds of revisions before painting begins. I'll share a composition sketch and color study for your approval. Once painting starts, major changes to composition aren't possible, so we ensure everything is finalized upfront.

Pricing & Payment

What payment methods do you accept?
Within India, I accept UPI, bank transfer, and other standard methods — details will be shared when you reach out. For international orders, payment options will be discussed based on your location.
Do you offer Cash on Delivery (COD)?
Currently, all orders are prepaid to ensure a smooth and committed process.
Is payment required upfront?
For Canvas Prints and Hand Painted pieces, full payment is required before work begins. For Commissions, a 50% advance is required, with the remaining balance due before dispatch. Payment details will be shared when you reach out.
What's the deposit structure for a commission?
Commissions require a 50% deposit upfront before work begins, with the remaining 50% due before dispatch.

Shipping & Delivery

Do you provide a tracking number once shipped?
Yes, once your order is dispatched you will receive a tracking number so you can follow your shipment in real time.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. Standard pricing currently applies only within India. For international orders, please contact me directly — shipping costs and timelines vary by destination.
Is the packaging safe for international shipping?
Yes, prints are packed securely in protective tubes or reinforced cardboard packaging.
When will I receive my order?
Prints ship within 10–15 days (subject to stock availability). Hand-painted works take 3–4 weeks to allow for painting and proper drying before quality checks. Commission timelines vary and will be shared during the discussion.

Returns, Damage & Support

Can I return or exchange a print?
As each piece is made to order, returns for change of mind are not accepted. However, if there is a quality issue or transit damage, I will ensure it is resolved. Damages must be reported with an unboxing video within 12 hours of delivery.
What if my piece is damaged in transit?
All pieces are carefully packed to prevent damage. In the unlikely event your artwork arrives damaged, photograph the packaging and artwork immediately upon unboxing and share it with me. An unboxing video within 12 hours is required. I will arrange a replacement or resolution as quickly as possible.

Care Instructions

How should I care for my fine art print?
  • Environment: Display prints indoors only, away from direct sunlight to prevent fading. Avoid areas with extreme changes in humidity, heat, or cold.
  • Cleaning: Display in a dust-free environment. Occasionally dust with a clean, soft brush or duster. Do not use cloth, water, or cleaning products.
  • Framing: Do not cover canvas prints with glass. Canvas needs to "breathe" to prevent moisture trap. If moisture does occur, place the frame in warm sunlight for one hour per day for a week.
